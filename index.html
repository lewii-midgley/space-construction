About us


Space Construction is an established and highly recommended house renovation company based in Wellington, New Zealand, offering full house renovation, bathroom renovation and kitchen renovation services. We will go above and beyond every step of the way to help you decide on a renovation plan that is truly for you and your family’s needs. We are here to make your house feel more like home ever before. Space Construction is a mixture of passion, effective results and personalization, engaging with the best in the construction industry to create an amazing client-focused renovation process. In an ever-changing industry, we’re all about challenging the status quo in providing the best possible service and options to our clients without compromising on quality.
